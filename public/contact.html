Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly do you respond to support requests?

We respond to all support requests within 24 hours during business days. Live chat provides instant responses during business hours.

Do you offer phone support?

Yes, phone support is available for Business and Enterprise plan customers, as well as for urgent technical issues.

Do you support multiple languages?

Our interface is available in English, Spanish, French, German, and Japanese. Support is primarily provided in English.

Can I request a feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing from users. Submit feature requests through our support channels or community forum.

Do you offer enterprise support?

Yes, enterprise customers receive dedicated account managers, priority support, and custom SLA agreements.

How do I report a security issue?

Security concerns should be reported immediately to security@quantumbaycloud.com. We have a dedicated security team for rapid response.
