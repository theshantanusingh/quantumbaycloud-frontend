Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing adjustments.

Is my data secure?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. We also offer two-factor authentication and advanced security features.

What happens to my files if I cancel?

You have 30 days after cancellation to download your files. After that period, your data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

Do you offer team discounts?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for teams of 10 or more users. Contact our sales team for custom enterprise pricing.

Are there mobile apps available?

Yes, we offer native mobile apps for iOS and Android, plus a responsive web interface that works on all devices.

What's your uptime guarantee?

We guarantee 99.9% uptime with a service level agreement. If we fall below this, you'll receive service credits.
